## PHASE 5: Interview Prep

### PM interview frameworks to master:
1. **CIRCLES method** — for product design questions
2. **RICE / ICE scoring** — for prioritization questions
3. **North Star Metric** — for strategy questions
4. **Jobs-to-be-Done** — for user research questions

### AI-specific questions to prepare:
- "How would you evaluate whether to build vs. buy an AI feature?"
- "A stakeholder wants to add AI to everything. How do you prioritize?"
- "How do you measure the success of an AI-powered feature?"
- "What's the difference between a rule-based system and an ML model? When would you use each?"
- "How do you handle AI hallucinations in a customer-facing product?"
